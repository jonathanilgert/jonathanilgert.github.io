How We Test

Batch records should be traceable to a real laboratory report.

The Wild Rose testing page is structured around the problem, laboratory partner records, method-specific reports, and per-batch Certificate of Analysis (COA) verification.

Preview guardrail: partner names, exact test menu, and any quantified market statistic stay unpublished until the client provides source confirmation and written permission.

The problem

Paperwork can look reassuring without being traceable.

A large share of peptide lab reports in circulation are difficult to trace to an identifiable laboratory, analyst, method, date, and lot record. This preview avoids unverified percentages until a source can be attributed.
