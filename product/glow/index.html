About this compound

About this compound

Glow is a three-peptide blend combining BPC-157, GHK-Cu, and TB-500 — three of the most-studied tissue-repair and skin-research compounds — in a single vial.

What it has been researched for

Research interest centres on combining peptides associated with angiogenesis (new blood-vessel growth), extracellular-matrix and skin remodelling, and cell migration, to study recovery- and skin-focused endpoints together. The underlying evidence is the preclinical literature of the individual components.

How it is thought to work

The components contribute complementary activities — BPC-157 (angiogenesis and cytoprotection), GHK-Cu (matrix remodelling and copper delivery), and TB-500 (actin regulation and cell migration, i.e. helping cells move to repair sites).

Regulatory status

Each component is a research compound, not an approved medicine; BPC-157, TB-500, and GHK-Cu are all part of the FDA’s 2026–2027 peptide-compounding review in the U.S. This does not change the blend’s research-use status in Canada — not for human consumption.

Product details

A single vial containing a 70 mg blend (BPC-157 10 mg, GHK-Cu 50 mg, TB-500 10 mg) as a lyophilised powder.
